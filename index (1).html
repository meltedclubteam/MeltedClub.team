Our Story

Inspired by late-night cravings, street culture, and social-media dessert trends, MELTEDCLUB combines rich melted chocolate, frozen bananas, and carefully selected toppings into something fun, addictive, and visually satisfying.

Our Mission

The brand was built for people who love bold flavors, aesthetic food, and modern dessert culture. More than just a snack, MELTEDCLUB is designed to feel like a lifestyle brand — playful, premium, and made to be shared.
